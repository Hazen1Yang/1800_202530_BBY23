The Start

We started this app to make a difference. Our goal: help people find meaningful careers and feel confident about their future. Pathfinder began as a BCIT project — but it grew fast because students like you needed real guidance.

Our Goal

We want to give everyone clarity. A career is a big decision — and most young people feel lost. Pathfinder helps you understand the job market, discover your strengths, and map out a realistic plan.

How We'll Do It

You start with a personal career survey. Then we match you with paths that fit your personality, skills, and goals. You can explore salaries, growth forecasts, work environments, and even connect with mentors.
