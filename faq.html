Frequently asked questions

Most common questions about using Pathfinder and the program match quiz.

How does the Program Match Quiz work?

The quiz asks about your interests and preferences and maps your answers to program categories. It provides suggested program areas to explore, not a definitive decision.

Do I need to sign in to save goals?

You can save goals locally without signing in, but signing in lets you sync goals across devices and keep them backed up.

Where does career data come from?

Career summaries are compiled from public sources such as WorkBC and Job Bank Canada. They are for guidance and may not reflect every regional nuance.

How can I suggest new features?

Contributions and feature ideas are welcome — please open an issue on the project repository or contact the team via the project lead.
