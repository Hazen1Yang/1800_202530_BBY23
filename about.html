About Pathfinder

Helping students discover meaningful careers and plan concrete steps to get there.

Our mission

Pathfinder helps learners find careers that fit their strengths, interests, and the job market. We combine personalised quizzes with practical resources so students can plan realistic next steps.

Why it exists

Many students feel uncertain about career choices. Pathfinder gives clear, accessible guidance and helps users track goals and opportunities that match their path.

What we offer

  • Program matching quiz
  • Career exploration and salary context
  • Goal tracking and task planning
  • Roadmaps to apply and prepare for programs

Team & credits

Built as a student project and improved by contributors. Thanks to BCIT and the open data sources like WorkBC and Job Bank for guiding the content.
